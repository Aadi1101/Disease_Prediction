Disease Prediction Using Machine Learning

Introduction:

Predicting diseases based on various symptoms is a critical application of machine learning and healthcare. This project focuses on developing a disease prediction model using a wide range of symptoms and medical indicators. The goal is to assist healthcare professionals in early disease diagnosis and treatment.

Dataset Description:

The dataset used in this study comprises anonymized patient data, including a multitude of symptoms and medical indicators. It contains features such as itching, skin rash, nodal skin eruptions, continuous sneezing, shivering, chills, joint pain, stomach pain, acidity, ulcers on tongue, muscle wasting, vomiting, burning micturition, spotting urination, fatigue, weight gain, anxiety, and numerous others.
